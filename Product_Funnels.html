How the funnel should work for each product

This is a category-specific funnel map for Jaipur electronics shoppers. AC and TVs need the most education and comparison. Fridges and washing machines need matching by household needs. Fans should stay fast, simple, and price-led.

Air conditioner

AC funnel: the deepest and most consultative

This should be a long-guidance funnel because the buyer cares about comfort, room fit, bill impact, installation, and service.

1. Trigger
Summer heat, discomfort, repeated cooling need
Need recognition100%
Often starts from weather, family discomfort, or replacing an old AC.
2. Fit
Room size, tonnage, inverter, star rating
Calculator84%
The user needs confidence that the AC is right for the room and usage pattern.
3. Compare
Brand, energy savings, noise, warranty
Shortlist74%
Comparison should reduce uncertainty on electricity bills, service quality, and features.
4. Trust
Demo, installation promise, reviews
Reassurance58%
This is where installation, demo, and warranty messaging should remove friction.
5. Convert
EMI, store visit, same-day purchase
Purchase46%
Close with EMI, delivery, installation timeline, and after-sales plan.

Need

Research

Risk

What will it do to my electricity bill? How much does installation cost?

Comparison

LG vs Daikin, Samsung vs Panasonic, price vs long-term savings.

Store

Cooling demo, noise comparison, expert advice, room matching.

After sale

Installation tracking, AMC, service reminders, warranty registration.

  • Store demo for final confidence

Bottom line

How to think about the funnel

AC and TV should have the most education, comparison, and reassurance. These are the products where calculators, demos, and consultations matter most.

Fast matching products

Fridge and washing machine should quickly map household needs to capacity and convenience. Fan should be the shortest journey of all.
